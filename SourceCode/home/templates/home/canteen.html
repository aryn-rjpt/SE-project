The college has a well-furnished canteen that spreads over 230 metres, accommodating around 100 people. Having a wide variety of menu at highly nominal prices without any compromises on the quality of the food, the canteen provides a bustling ambience for the students, staff members and visitors with its spacious, hygienic and cozy space. A new venture called ‘Shakes and More’ is also instituted on the campus to offer an extensive variety of food and beverages. The canteen is efficiently administered by a Canteen Committee appointed by the College Staff Council who ensure to make it one of the finest and hygienic canteens in the University.
